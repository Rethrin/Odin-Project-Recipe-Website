Black Beans: The Cuban Way

Velvety, perfectly cooked black beans, infused with the flavors of tangy lime, smoky cumin, and aromatic garlic. The beans are simmered to perfection with onions, bell peppers, and fresh herbs until they are creamy and bursting with flavor. As you take a bite, you'll be transported to the sunny beaches of Cuba, where the scent of the sea and the sound of salsa music fills the air. This versatile dish is perfect on its own as a vegetarian meal, or as a side dish to complement grilled meats or seafood. It's hearty, healthy, and absolutely mouthwatering, and sure to be a hit with your family and friends. So why not bring a taste of Cuba into your kitchen today and give this black beans recipe a try?

Ingredients Required:

  • 1 pound black beans, soaked overnight
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 large eggs
  • Chopped fresh cilantro or parsley (for garnish)

The Process:

  1. Drain the soaked black beans and rinse them well. Set them aside.
  2. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and green bell pepper, and saut√© until the onion is translucent, about 5-7 minutes..
  3. Add the minced garlic, ground cumin, dried oregano, and bay leaf to the pot, and stir to combine. Cook for another 1-2 minutes, until the garlic is fragrant.
  4. Add the tomato paste to the pot, and stir to combine with the vegetables and spices. Cook for another 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add the black beans and chicken or vegetable broth to the pot, and stir to combine. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and simmer, covered, for 1-1 1/2 hours, or until the beans are tender and the liquid has thickened.
  6. Remove the bay leaf from the pot, and stir in the lime juice. Season the black beans with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve the Cuban-style black beans hot, garnished with chopped fresh cilantro or parsley if desired.

    8. This recipe makes approximately 6 servings. Enjoy the delicious taste of Cuba with these flavorful and hearty black beans!
